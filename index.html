Two Frameworks.
One National Mission.

My work centers on two proprietary systems designed to solve specific, high-cost problems in U.S. industrial infrastructure. Both frameworks are active, peer-reviewed, and field-tested.

Framework 01

AOMS

The Automated Operations Management System delivers predictive maintenance for U.S. small and medium-sized food manufacturing enterprises. Structured around ISO 13374 condition monitoring architecture, AOMS reduces unplanned downtime by 40 to 45% and has improved Overall Equipment Effectiveness from 68% to 87% across field deployments, sustaining that performance over a six-year operational record.

Framework 02

ICM

The Integrated Competitiveness Model maps six operational domains to bridge Industry 4.0 and Industry 5.0 adoption in manufacturing. ICM gives plant operators a structured path to digital transformation without replacing existing infrastructure. It targets efficiency gaps that cost U.S. manufacturers billions annually.
