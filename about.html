Professional Summary

Engineering Expertise at Industrial Scale

Richard Kudjo Akrong is an electrical engineer and industrial automation specialist with over 15 years of field experience in critical infrastructure protection. As former Technical Managing Director at Casabeck Engineering Services, he led 28 projects across 13 countries, with significant deployments in food and beverage manufacturing, edible oil processing, and SCADA-controlled industrial systems.

His work spans PLC, SCADA, and DCS system design, Industrial IoT integration, renewable energy and microgrid engineering, and cybersecurity architecture for operational technology environments. He developed two proprietary frameworks, AOMS and ICM, designed to improve operational reliability and accelerate Industry 4.0 adoption in U.S. food manufacturing infrastructure.

He holds IEEE Senior Member and ISA Senior Member status alongside IECEx and OSHA certifications. He serves as a peer reviewer for ISA Transactions (Elsevier) and as a judge for the Virginia State Science and Engineering Fair, reflecting his commitment to advancing the U.S. engineering knowledge base.
