PUB-01 International Journal of Innovative Science and Research Technology (IJISRT) October 2025
Automation in the Modern Power Distribution System: Improving Energy Security and Efficiency

This paper examines automation as a solution for improving energy security, operational efficiency, and grid reliability in modern U.S. power distribution systems. It covers automated transformers, Medium Voltage Distribution (MVD) systems, Low Voltage (LV) panels, and Power Factor Correction (PFC) systems, and demonstrates how real-time data analytics and intelligent control algorithms reduce technical losses, cut outage duration, and improve field safety. Findings from the author's own industrial deployments are presented alongside federal energy modernization data, placing the technical contributions directly within the context of U.S. grid policy priorities.

PUB-02 Global Journal of Engineering and Technology (GJET) October 2025
Automation as a Driver of Sustainable Development: Aligning Industrial Energy Use with National Climate Goals

This paper explores how industrial automation and digitalization enable the United States to meet its greenhouse gas emission reduction targets while preserving industrial competitiveness. Drawing on IoT sensor integration, predictive analytics, and intelligent process optimization, the paper demonstrates that environmental performance and economic output are not in conflict. Case studies from industrial installations show measurable reductions in emissions, energy use, and production waste, all achieved through automation rather than operational slowdown. The paper directly addresses U.S. Department of Energy emission reduction priorities and positions engineering expertise as central to national climate strategy.
